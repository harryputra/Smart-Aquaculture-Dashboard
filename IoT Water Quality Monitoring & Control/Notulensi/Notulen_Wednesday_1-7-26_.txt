Water Level Control From Water Quality Monitoring

\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\
Alat dan Bahan :
Input
   A. Water Quality	
	1. Turbidity sensor = kekeruhan air kolam
	2. Dissolved oxygen sensor = kadar oksigen air
	3. pH sensor = kadar asam basa air
   B. Water Quantity
	4. Ultrasonic = ketinggian air
	
Output
1. Relay
2. Solenoid Valve 2 unit ( 1/2 inch dan 2 inch )
	A. Solenoid Input Air
	B. Solenoid Kuras Air

\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\
Sistem Permintaan User
1. [MANUAL] Pengurasan air langsung dari user via web (digital logic only on off)
2. [AUTO] Pengurasan air dan pengisian air berdasarkan parameter nilai yang dihasilkan oleh kombinasi sensor diatas


Alur Kerja Sistem Untuk Poin 2 [AUTO]
Water Quantity
1. if ultrasonik deteksi level air dibawah batas minimum then pompa on | dan sebaliknya

Water Quality
1. if turbidity, pH, oxygen dibawah batas minimum then valve keluaran on dan balik ke poin 1 -> harapnya biar airnya jadi jernih kembali

\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\
Nilai dummy

Kolam min : 30 cm
Kolam max : 80 cm

Nilai sensor :
1. pH min 3 max 6 (real)
2. turbity min 120 max 180 (ADC)
3. Oxygen - PR teh Adin

1. Point 2 = pembacaan sensor min max
2. Time valve on/off based on pembacaan ultrasonic (point 1)
